WEEKEND SOLUTIONS
Event format (1kg boxes) — ready meals in large boxes for parties, birthdays, office meetups. Avg check 1,200–2,000 RUB vs 250 RUB standard. Low competition in Samara.
Meal Prep Sunday — weekly ration delivery on Sundays. Subscribers receive 5 dishes. Fixed revenue without aggregator.
B2B weekly pre-orders — corporate client pays for 5 workdays upfront on Friday. Production Sat–Sun loads the kitchen without retail demand.
P&L SCENARIOS
How it's calculated: Month = 22 weekdays + 8 weekend days × weekend factor. Net revenue = Gross Revenue × (1 − weighted avg commission). EBITDA = GP − all fixed costs (payroll + rent + utilities + marketing + other).
